Sunrise and Sunset

Sunrise

Sunrise refers to both the crossing of the horizon (rise) by the sun and the time of this event in the daily course of the sun as well as the celestial phenomenon as a whole with all its (including meteorological) phenomena..

Sunset

Sunset is the disappearance of the sun below the horizon as well as the time of its setting in the daily course of the sun and the celestial phenomenon as such with all its phenomena..

Day and Night

Day and night have a simple cause: the earth rotates. When our location on Earth is pointing towards the sun, it is light, i.e. day. ... We see the sun going down and it's getting dark. The moon also rises and sets for exactly the same reason: because the earth rotates..
